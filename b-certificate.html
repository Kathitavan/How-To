Basic Requirements for Birth Certificate

1. Required Documents

You need to provide the following documents:

  • Proof of Birth (e.g., Hospital Discharge Summary, Birth Notification, etc.)
  • Proof of Identity of Parents (e.g., Aadhaar Card, Passport, etc.)
  • Proof of Address (e.g., Utility Bill, Bank Statement, etc.)
  • Proof of Citizenship (e.g., Passport, Aadhaar Card, etc.)

2. Application Process

The steps to apply for a Birth Certificate:

  • Visit the nearest Birth Certificate Office or apply online through the official portal.
  • Fill out the application form with the necessary details.
  • Submit the required documents for verification.
  • Attend any required interviews or follow-up procedures.
  • Receive your Birth Certificate at your registered address or download it online.

3. Eligibility

Eligibility criteria are usually based on providing proof of birth and parentage. This can be applied for immediately after birth or later with valid documents.

4. Cost

Applying for a Birth Certificate is generally free, but some jurisdictions may charge a nominal fee for processing.

5. Tracking

You can track your Birth Certificate application status online using the application number provided.
