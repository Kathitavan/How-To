Basic Requirements for Voter ID

1. Required Documents

You need to provide the following documents:

  • Proof of Identity (e.g., Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, etc.)
  • Proof of Address (e.g., Utility Bill, Bank Statement, Rent Agreement, etc.)
  • Proof of Age (e.g., Birth Certificate, School Leaving Certificate, etc.)

2. Enrollment Process

The steps to apply for a Voter ID:

  • Visit the nearest Election Commission office or apply online through the National Voter Services Portal (NVSP).
  • Fill out the application form (Form 6).
  • Submit the required documents for verification.
  • Get your photograph taken, if applying in person.
  • Receive an acknowledgment slip with your application number.

3. Age Requirement

Individuals who are 18 years of age or older are eligible to apply for a Voter ID.


4. Cost

Applying for a Voter ID is free of charge.


5. Tracking

You can track your Voter ID application status online using the application number provided in the acknowledgment slip.
