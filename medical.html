Basic Requirements for Medical Certificate

1. Required Documents

You need to provide the following documents:

  • Medical Reports (e.g., Recent medical examination reports, lab test results, etc.)
  • Doctor's Prescription (if applicable)
  • Proof of Identity (e.g., Aadhaar Card, Passport, Voter ID, etc.)
  • Passport-sized Photographs

Requirements for Medical Certificate

2. Application Process

The steps to apply for a Medical Certificate:

  • Visit a certified medical practitioner or authorized medical facility.
  • Undergo the required medical examination.
  • Submit the necessary documents for verification.
  • Pay any applicable fees.
  • Receive your Medical Certificate after the examination is complete.

3. Eligibility

The eligibility criteria for obtaining a Medical Certificate depend on the purpose, such as employment, travel, or education.

4. Cost

The cost of obtaining a Medical Certificate varies depending on the medical facility and the type of examination required.

5. Tracking

Tracking is usually not required for a Medical Certificate, as it is issued immediately after the examination.
