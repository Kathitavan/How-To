Basic Requirements for Passport Card

1. Required Documents

You need to provide the following documents:

  • Proof of Identity (e.g., Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, PAN Card, etc.)
  • Proof of Address (e.g., Utility Bill, Bank Statement, Rent Agreement, etc.)
  • Proof of Citizenship (e.g., Birth Certificate, Certificate of Citizenship, etc.)
  • Proof of Date of Birth (e.g., Birth Certificate, School Leaving Certificate, etc.)

2. Enrollment Process

The steps to apply for a Passport Card:

  • Visit the nearest Passport Seva Kendra (PSK) or apply online through the Passport Seva portal.
  • Fill out the application form.
  • Submit the required documents for verification.
  • Schedule an appointment for biometric data collection (photograph, fingerprints, and signature).
  • Receive an acknowledgment slip with your application number.

3. Age Requirement

Individuals of any age can apply for a Passport Card. Minors require parental consent.


4. Cost

Applying for a Passport Card involves a fee, which varies based on the type of service selected (normal or tatkal).


5. Tracking

You can track your Passport Card application status online using the application number provided in the acknowledgment slip.
