Basic Requirements for Aadhaar Card

1. Required Documents

You need to provide the following documents:

  • Proof of Identity (e.g., Passport, PAN Card, Voter ID, etc.)
  • Proof of Address (e.g., Utility Bill, Bank Statement, Ration Card, etc.)
  • Proof of Date of Birth (e.g., Birth Certificate, SSLC Certificate, Passport, etc.)

2. Enrollment Process

The steps to apply for Aadhaar:

  • Visit the nearest Aadhaar Enrollment Center.
  • Fill out the application form.
  • Provide your documents for verification.
  • Get your photograph, fingerprints, and iris scan taken.
  • Receive an acknowledgment slip with your enrollment number.

3. Age Requirement

Anyone of any age can apply for Aadhaar, including children.

4. Cost

Applying for Aadhaar is free of charge.

5. Tracking

You can track your Aadhaar application status online using the enrollment number provided in the acknowledgment slip.
