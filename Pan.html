Basic Requirements for PAN Card

1. Required Documents

You need to provide the following documents:

  • Proof of Identity (e.g., Aadhaar Card, Passport, Voter ID, etc.)
  • Proof of Address (e.g., Utility Bill, Bank Statement, Rent Agreement, etc.)
  • Proof of Date of Birth (e.g., Birth Certificate, Passport, etc.)

2. Enrollment Process

The steps to apply for a PAN Card:

  • Visit the nearest PAN Card Enrollment Center or apply online.
  • Fill out the application form (Form 49A for individuals).
  • Provide your documents for verification.
  • Get your photograph and signature taken if applying offline.
  • Receive an acknowledgment slip with your application number.

3. Age Requirement

Individuals of any age can apply for a PAN Card.


4. Cost

Applying for a PAN Card involves a nominal fee (around INR 107 for Indian citizens).


5. Tracking

You can track your PAN Card application status online using the application number provided in the acknowledgment slip.
