Basic Requirements for Passbook

Bank Passbook Information

1. Required Documents

You need to provide the following documents:

  • Proof of Identity (e.g., Aadhar Card, Passport, Voter ID)
  • Proof of Address (e.g., Utility Bill, Ration Card, Bank Statement)
  • Recent Passport-sized Photograph
  • Bank Form (completed and signed)

2. Application Process

The steps to obtain a Bank Passbook:

  • Visit the nearest bank branch.
  • Fill out the application form for a passbook.
  • Submit the required documents.
  • Get your photograph taken if required.
  • Receive your passbook once the application is processed.

3. Eligibility

Eligibility requirements vary by bank. Generally, you must have an active savings or current account.

4. Cost

Issuing a passbook is generally free of charge, but some banks may charge a nominal fee for additional services.

5. Tracking

You can track the status of your passbook application by contacting your bank branch or checking through the bank's online portal.
