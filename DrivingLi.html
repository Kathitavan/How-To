Basic Requirements for Driving License

1. Required Documents

You need to provide the following documents:

  • Proof of Identity (e.g., Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, etc.)
  • Proof of Address (e.g., Utility Bill, Bank Statement, Rent Agreement, etc.)
  • Proof of Age (e.g., Birth Certificate, School Leaving Certificate, etc.)
  • Driving School Certificate (for transport vehicles)

2. Enrollment Process

The steps to apply for a Driving License:

  • Visit the nearest Regional Transport Office (RTO) or apply online through the Parivahan website.
  • Fill out the application form (Form 4 for learners, Form 8 for a new license).
  • Submit the required documents for verification.
  • Complete the learnerâ€™s license test (if applicable).
  • Pass the driving test for the vehicle type you are applying for (two-wheeler or four-wheeler).
  • Receive your driving license after passing the test.

3. Age Requirement

Individuals must be at least 18 years old to apply for a driving license for two-wheelers and four-wheelers.


4. Cost

There are fees associated with applying for a driving license, including the application fee, test fee, and license issuance fee.


5. Tracking

You can track your driving license application status online using the application number provided by the RTO.
