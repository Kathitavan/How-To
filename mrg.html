Basic Requirements for Marriage Certificate

1. Required Documents

You need to provide the following documents:

  • Proof of Marriage (e.g., Marriage Registration Document, Marriage Invitation Card, etc.)
  • Proof of Identity (e.g., Aadhaar Card, Passport, Voter ID, etc.)
  • Proof of Address (e.g., Utility Bill, Bank Statement, etc.)
  • Proof of Citizenship (e.g., Passport, Aadhaar Card, etc.)
  • Photographs (e.g., Recent Passport-sized Photos of both spouses)

2. Application Process

The steps to apply for a Marriage Certificate:

  • Visit the nearest Marriage Registration Office or apply online through the official portal.
  • Fill out the application form with the necessary details.
  • Submit the required documents for verification.
  • Attend any required interviews or follow-up procedures.
  • Receive your Marriage Certificate at your registered address or download it online.

3. Eligibility

Eligibility criteria may vary depending on your location. Generally, both parties must be legally eligible for marriage and provide required documentation.

4. Cost

Applying for a Marriage Certificate may involve a nominal fee for processing and issuance.

5. Tracking

You can track your Marriage Certificate application status online using the application number provided.
